Protection & Security

Personal Safety Tips

  • Always share your travel details with a trusted contact.
  • Avoid isolated areas at night.
  • Keep emergency numbers saved on your phone.

Legal Rights

Every woman has the right to safety and dignity. Contact police or helpline numbers if you face harassment or violence.

Digital Safety

Use strong passwords, avoid oversharing personal details online, and beware of scams or unknown links.

Essential Items to Carry

  • Mobile phone with emergency contacts saved
  • Whistle or personal alarm for alerting others
  • Small torch or keychain flashlight
  • Mini first aid kit (band-aids, antiseptic wipes)
  • Pen and small notepad for notes or emergency info
  • Portable charger/power bank
  • ID and some cash in case of emergencies
  • Pepper spray (if legal in your area)
