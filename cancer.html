Cancer Awareness

What is Cancer?

Cancer occurs when abnormal cells grow uncontrollably and spread to other parts of the body. Early detection increases the chances of successful treatment.

Common Types in Women

  • Breast Cancer
  • Cervical Cancer
  • Ovarian Cancer
  • Skin Cancer
  • Blood Cancer

Symptoms to Watch

  • Unexplained lumps or swelling
  • Persistent fatigue or weight loss
  • Abnormal bleeding or discharge
  • Changes in skin, moles, or breasts

Prevention & Healthy Lifestyle

  • Maintain a balanced diet and exercise regularly
  • Avoid tobacco and limit alcohol consumption
  • Use sun protection and avoid prolonged UV exposure
  • Get vaccinated for HPV (helps prevent cervical cancer)

Screening & Early Detection

Regular check-ups, mammograms, Pap smears, and self-exams help detect cancer early, increasing treatment success rates.

Treatment Options

Treatment depends on cancer type and stage: surgery, chemotherapy, radiation therapy, immunotherapy, or a combination.

Support & Awareness

Joining support groups, staying informed, and spreading awareness helps women fight cancer and reduces stigma.
